Analytics · Subscription · Streaming

Netflix Subscription Analysis & Prediction

Applied K-Means clustering to Netflix user data to surface three distinct subscriber segments — Casual Users, Committed Streamers, and High-Value Subscribers — enabling targeted retention and upsell strategies. Calculated Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) across the user base and identified the top 25% of revenue contributors. Tracked acquisition trends to reveal two major growth peaks in 2022, with a Tableau dashboard built for stakeholder consumption.

Product · Consumer · Feature Ideation

Social Shuffle — Spotify Feature Concept

Identified a dual pain point in Spotify: shuffle repetition frustrating 1 in 3 users, and social features (Blend, Friend Activity) sitting underutilised. Proposed Social Shuffle — a collaborative, real-time shuffle mode that turns solo listening into a shared experience. Grounded in user research showing 77% of Gen Z crave music-based connection.
