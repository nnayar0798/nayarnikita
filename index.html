Analytics · Subscription Intelligence · Streaming

Netflix Subscription Analysis & Prediction

Applied K-Means clustering to Netflix subscriber data to uncover three actionable segments: Casual Users (low revenue, short tenure), Committed Streamers (mid-tier, sticky), and High-Value Subscribers (high CLV, long duration). Calculated Customer Lifetime Value across the full user base and identified the top 25% of revenue contributors — pinpointing the most critical users to retain. Tracked monthly acquisition trends revealing two major growth spikes in 2022, with insight into the drop-off periods between peaks. Built a Tableau dashboard for stakeholder consumption.
